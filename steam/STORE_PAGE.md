### Five Games, Five Worlds

**Gomoku (Five in a Row)**
Classic 15x15 or 19x19 board. Play with standard rules or the strategic Renju variant with forbidden-move enforcement. Features complete opening theory with 3-move strategic responses and real-time threat detection. AI adapts search depth based on game phase — from random openings to deep tactical reading.

**Go (Weiqi / Baduk)**
The ancient game of territory on 9x9, 13x13, or 19x19 boards. Full Chinese and Japanese scoring support with accurate life-and-death evaluation. AI uses 2-ply minimax with Monte Carlo territory evaluation and transposition tables for stronger play. Post-game territory visualization helps you understand where the game was won.

**Chess**
Standard 8x8 chess with full rule enforcement: castling, en passant, promotion, and checkmate detection. AI uses minimax with alpha-beta pruning at depth 5 on hard mode, enhanced with MVV-LVA piece valuation and killer move heuristics. Piece-square tables drive positional evaluation.

**Xiangqi (Chinese Chess)**
9x10 board with river mechanics, palace restrictions, and cannon jump rules. Chariot/Horse/Cannon tactical play with AI depth-5 search with killer move heuristic on hard mode. Positional bonuses reward controlling key intersections and threatening the opponent's palace.

**Junqi (Luzhanqi / Military Chess)**
13x13 board with unique flip-reveal mechanics. Military rank hierarchy, railway mobility, camp usage, and flag protection strategies. AI evaluates defensive flag protection and optimal reveal timing. A game of hidden information and calculated risk.
