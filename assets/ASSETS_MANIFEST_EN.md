## 📋 Asset Creation Checklist

### Design Principles
1. **Visual Consistency** - Use consistent color scheme and visual style
2. **Brand Identity** - Highlight Go/Gomoku core elements
3. **Clarity** - Clear and recognizable even at small sizes
4. **Professionalism** - Meet Steam platform quality standards

### Color Scheme Suggestions
- Primary: Deep blue or dark gray background
- Accent: Gold or white stone elements
- Secondary: Wooden board tones

### File Naming Convention
- Use lowercase letters and underscores
- Avoid spaces and special characters
- Use descriptive names
